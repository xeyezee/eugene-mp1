Beef pares

Beef Pares is a popular Filipino dish that combines tender beef simmered in a flavorful soy based sauce,served with garlic rice and a side of beef broth.

Tapsilog

Tapsilog is a popular Filipino food that consist of three main components, tapa. sinangag, and itlog. "Tapa" refers to thinly sliced or marinated beef that is typically sweet, savory, and slightly tangy in flavor.

Longsilog

Longsilog is a popular Filipino food that consist of three main components longganisa, sinangag, and itlog. Longganisa refers to the sweet or savory Filipino sausage.
